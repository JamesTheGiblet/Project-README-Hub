## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser.
2. Follow the 6-step interactive questionnaire, filling in your project's details.
3. Choose your preferred AI model and writing tone.
4. Click **Generate** and watch the AI craft your README.
5. Copy the final Markdown or download the file.
